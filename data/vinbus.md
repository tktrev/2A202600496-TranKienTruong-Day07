# VinBus — Overview and Data

## 1. Introduction

**VinBus** is an electric public transportation company in Vietnam operated by **VinBus Ecology Transport Services LLC**, a subsidiary of **Vingroup**.  

The company was established in **2019** with the goal of developing a **modern, environmentally friendly urban public transportation system** based entirely on electric buses. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

VinBus represents one of Vietnam’s earliest large-scale attempts to electrify public transit and reduce urban air and noise pollution.

## 4. Deployment History

### Hanoi (Pilot & Early Deployment — 2021)
- First smart electric buses tested in Hanoi streets in **May 2021**
- Initial routes connected major urban areas such as:
  - Hao Nam → Vinhomes Ocean Park
  - Times City → Central administrative zone
  - Ho Tay Water Park → Vinhomes Smart City :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

The system was first tested inside Vingroup urban complexes before integrating into the city’s public transport network.

## 10. Challenges

Common challenges for electric bus systems include:

- High upfront infrastructure cost
- Charging logistics and battery range limits
- Dependence on government subsidies common in public transport
- Operational profitability constraints

These issues are typical for early-stage electrified transit worldwide.
